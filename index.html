频率背后的故事 BEHIND THE FREQUENCY

如今，我持有B类执照，正式接入这个全球无线电网络。当耳机里传来几千公里外陌生而温暖的声线，这不仅仅是物理上的电磁波震动，更是两个灵魂在虚空中的瞬间纠缠。这偌大的地球，在电台的通联中被折叠成一方小小的听筒。

It began with static. Before smartphones ruled the world, I used a household satellite dish as a makeshift antenna. Crude, yes, but effective. The faint broadcasts seeping through the noise were like laser beams piercing the silence of my childhood.

Now, licensed and online, I connect to the global network. When a stranger's voice cuts through the static from thousands of kilometers away, it's more than electromagnetic waves—it's a momentary connection of souls across the void.
